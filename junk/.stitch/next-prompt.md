A high-fidelity **Inventory Dashboard** for Pantry Guardian. 

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Creative North Star**: "The Kitchen Aesthetic" (Editorial, Fresh, Authoritative)
- **Palette**: Primary Fresh Green (#2E7D32), Earthy Ivory (#FAFAF5), Warm Amber (#FFA000) for alerts, and Deep Charcoal (#2C2C2C) for text.
- **Typography**: Noto Serif for headlines (Prestige/Authority), Manrope for body text (Modern/Scalable).
- **Styles**: 12px rounded corners (rounded-2xl). Tonal shifts (Earthy Ivory vs Off-White) instead of borders.

**Dashboard Structure:**
1. **Sidebar/Nav**: Vertical navigation with icons (Inventory, Recipes, Insights, Shopping List). Logged-in user profile at the bottom.
2. **Top Bar**: Search bar ("Search your pantry...") and "Add Item" primary button (Lush Green).
3. **Overview Hero**: A large Noto Serif greeting: "Good Morning, Chef. Your kitchen is 85% fresh." A subtle progress ring showing overall freshness.
4. **Primary Content (Inventory List)**:
   - "Expiring Soon" Horizontal Scroll: Large, beautiful cards for items like "Organic Bananas" (Warm Amber alert) or "Whole Milk".
   - "Full Inventory" Grid: Categorized list (Produce, Dairy, Pantry) using the editorial grid style. Each item shows a name, expiry date, and a "Freshness" indicator.
5. **Insights Widget (Right Sidebar/Panel)**: "Suggested for tonight: Banana Bread" with a small recipe thumbnail.
